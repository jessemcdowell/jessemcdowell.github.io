About Me

I’m a full-stack software developer that loves designing and building top-notch software. I am a bit stronger in the back-end. I’ve had the pleasure of building software for scientists, doctors, technicians, and gamers.

I care a lot about extensibility, maintainability, scalability, and security. I can work with C#, Java, Javascript, Typescript, Python, Ruby, and several other technologies.

I live and work in Vancouver BC.
